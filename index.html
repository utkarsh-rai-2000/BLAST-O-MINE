Hello and welcome!

Meet the largest independent blasting module

The web design offers you various features and have been tested for some coal mines. The calculations are based on various empirical equations supporting blast designs such as Fraenkel,Andersen, Pearse etc. The software is majorly based on the methodology suggested by Langefors and Kihlstrom.

What we offer

The developed software is user friendly and easy to use. As the software is developed based on the empirical relationship, the software has limited utilization. However, with the invented new exxplosives, the drilling and blasting pattern must be updated. The calculations are based on various important parameters such as rock parameters, explosive parameters and bench. Listed below is the complete list of parameters and future development of this software.

  • Mechanical property of rock
  • Pit geometry
  • Explosive characteristics
  • Spacing of the hole
  • Cost calculations to be intoduced.
  • Blasting visuals to be introduced.
  • A database to make it work faster.
  • Open source contribution is highly appreciated.
